Our Story

MITCH is a footwear store that offers a wide selection of local and global brands such as Ortuseight, Brodo, Adidas, and Nike. The name "MITCH" comes from the initials of its founders: Misael, Titan, Bima, and Chelsea. This store was created as part of a first-semester final project for the Introduction to Information Technology course, and also serves as a platform to express our creativity in design and technology.
